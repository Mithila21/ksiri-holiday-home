About the Villa

Tranquility meets luxury on the shores of Sri Lanka's pristine lake waters

Nestled among the lush mangroves of Balapitiya, K’siri Holiday Home is a serene lakeside sanctuary in southern Sri Lanka. Our villa offers the perfect blend of modern comfort and natural beauty, where panoramic water views and gentle breezes create an atmosphere of pure tranquility.

Designed for families, couples, and nature lovers, we provide a private escape from the hustle of everyday life. Come experience authentic Sri Lankan hospitality in a peaceful setting where time slows down and relaxation is the only priority.
